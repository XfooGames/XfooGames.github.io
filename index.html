Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal data from users of the App. The App does not require any personal information to function, and we do not collect, store, or share any user data.

Permissions

The App requires the following permissions to function correctly:

  • android.permission.CHANGE_WIFI_MULTICAST_STATE: This permission allows the app to change Wi-Fi multicast settings, which may be necessary for certain multiplayer features in the future.
  • android.permission.INTERNET: This permission allows the app to access the internet for online features and updates.
  • android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS: This permission allows the app to send notifications to users.
  • android.hardware.wifi: This feature allows the app to utilize Wi-Fi capabilities on the device.

Local Storage

The App uses the device's local storage to save and retrieve data necessary for its functionality. This data is stored only on your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties. This data may include:

  • Game progress
  • User settings and preferences
